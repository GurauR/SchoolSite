About Our School

Our School was founded in 1923 with the mission of providing students with a rigorous and well-rounded education that prepares them for success in the 21st century.

Today, we continue to uphold that mission, offering a range of academic programs and extracurricular activities that help students develop their skills, interests, and passions.

Our Faculty

Our faculty members are experienced educators who are committed to helping students achieve their full potential. They come from diverse backgrounds and bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the classroom.

Our Programs

We offer a range of academic programs to meet the needs of our students, from Advanced Placement courses to vocational programs in fields such as healthcare and engineering. Our extracurricular activities are also designed to help students explore their interests and develop their talents.
